About US

Accoal Industries is one of the leading manufacturers, exporters and supplier of Coconut shell charcoal in INDIA. We started our business in INDIA from 2001 onwards. We assure 60 to 70 % carbon content in all of our products.

We are distributing our products to almost all the reputed carbon and casting companies in INDIA. Our production capacity is around 500 to 1000 ton per month and we are actively working on to increase this day by day. We already started our exporting to Turkey and UAE and we aim to export our product all over Europe and Asia.

With more than 20 years of experience in the research and development, we can provide coconut shell based charcoal for the varied needs of our clients all over the world.

OUR PRODUCTS

Accoal Industries produce and sell a broad range types of granular activated carbon, activated carbon pellets, activated carbon powder, impregnated activated carbon and so on.

Coconut Shell Charcoal

These charcoals are made from processed coconut shell and are a great substitute for lump charcoal. Apart from imparting a smoky flavour, the charcoal burn hotter, longer, don’t spark, emit less smoke.

Coconut Shell Charcoal Granules

Coconut Shell Charcoal Granules are powdered form of Coconut charcoals. These charcoals are made from processed coconut shell and are a great substitute for lump charcoal. Apart from imparting a smoky flavour, the charcoal burn hotter, longer, don’t spark, emit less smoke.

Granular Activated Carbon

Granular activated carbon is mainly made from coconut shell, nut shell and coal, and is processed through series production process. The appearance is black amorphous particles; it has developed pore structure, good adsorption performance, high mechanical strength, easy repeated regeneration, low cost, etc.

Activated Carbon Pellets

Activated carbon pellets (also called pelletized activated carbon) is made from high-quality coal or coconut shell by extruding the activated carbon into cylindrical particles with a diameter range of 0.9 to 8mm. It's highly active surface area and make it dropped from the air and airflow choice of various pollutants.

Powdered Activated Carbon

Powdered activated carbon is made from high-quality wood chips and coal, and is processed through a series of production processes. Powder activated carbon has the advantages of fast filtration speed, good adsorption performance, strong decolorization and deodorization ability, economical and durable, etc.

Special Activated Carbon

Special activated carbon refers to the activated carbon that is different from the general use of water treatment and conventional gas adsorption, and is applicable to the high-tech field and new industrial applications, including ultra-high iodine value, ultra-low ash content, ultracapacitor activated carbon and other special applications.
